THEME

Do you ever ponder upon how vast our oceans actually are? They encompass a multitude of unexplainable spectacles, from underwater waterfalls to volcanoes, from deep-sea giants to bioluminescent microbes, from the remains of ancient civilizations to even the possibility of a highly advanced one! These infinite possibilities present the perfect opportunity for inquisitive minds to explore!

At the 26th edition of Techfest, we will take a deep dive into this abyssal mystery and explore the unfathomable possibilities that lie beneath our oceans. In this Submerged Sojourn, come join us in this expedition through a mystical realm!
